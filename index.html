About Us


MARKETSCOPE is, a NYC based, non-traditional talent acquisition company that specializes matching emerging tech talent into rewarding positions with diversity-seeking tech companies. Our mission is to close the breech of unemployment for non-traditional candidates by providing employers with highly screened and qualified non-traditional candidates from the ages 18-65.


We screen, interview, vet, and arm prospective job-seekers with the tools and platforms they need to demonstrate their qualifications on the job. Then hand-pick and present only the most brilliant and eligible applicants for our talent pool to our exclusive engagement partners, effortlessly taking all of the guesswork out of the HR process, so that the focus is simply business!
